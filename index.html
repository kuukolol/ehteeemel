PWD
Person with Disability

Details about PWD

  • A person with a disability (PWD) refers to someone with a physical, mental, or sensory impairment that hinders them from living a normal life.
  • RA 7277 Also known as the "Magna Carta for Disabled Persons", provides rehabilitation and self-development of disabled persons.
  • PWDs get benefits like discounts, priority lanes, and job.
  • They can apply for a PWD ID to access these benefits easily.

How should we deal with them?

  1. Treat them with respect and kindness.
  2. Offer help, but always ask first.
  3. Give them space and patience in public places.
  4. Focus on their abilities, not their disabilities.
